About me

I am a Ph.D. student co-trained at the Dornsife Spatial Sciences Institute, Department of Sociology & Keck Department of Population and Public Health Sciences at the University of Southern California[cite: 8]. Advised by Dr. John P. Wilson and Dr. Megan M. Herting [cite: 6], my research focuses on how physical and cultural environment interact and influence mental health. My research interests sit at the intersection of Health Geography, Environmental Perception, Mental Health, and GeoAI Applications[cite: 7].
